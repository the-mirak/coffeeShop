Our Story

Crafting exceptional coffee experiences since 2010

The Brew Haven Journey

Brew Haven began as a small passion project in 2010, born from the belief that coffee is more than just a beverage – it's an experience that brings people together. Our founder, Emma Clarke, traveled across continents to learn from master roasters and baristas, bringing home a vision of creating a space where exceptional coffee meets genuine community.

Our Beans

We source our beans directly from small-scale farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, ensuring fair compensation and sustainable practices. Each batch is carefully selected for its unique flavor profile and roasted in small batches at our local roastery to bring out the distinct characteristics of every origin.

Every cup tells a story – of the land where the beans were grown, the hands that harvested them, and the care with which they were roasted and brewed.

Our Values

Sustainability

From compostable packaging to energy-efficient equipment, we strive to minimize our environmental footprint in every aspect of our business.

Community

We believe in creating spaces where people connect. Our shop hosts local events, showcases local artists, and partners with neighborhood initiatives.

Craftsmanship

We approach coffee as an art form. Our baristas undergo extensive training, continuously refining their skills to deliver the perfect cup every time.

Our Journey

2010

The first Brew Haven opens in a small corner shop with just three tables and a vision.

2013

We establish direct trade relationships with farmers in Ethiopia and Colombia.

2016

Our in-house roastery opens, allowing us to control quality from bean to cup.

2019

Brew Haven achieves carbon neutrality through comprehensive sustainability initiatives.

2023

We launch our online store, bringing the Brew Haven experience to coffee lovers nationwide.
